Booting scheme is defined config file. put in boot option, e.g:

config=http://10.0.2.2/bootstrap/bootone

and the content of bootone is as follow:

###################################
TARGET=auto
IP=dhcp
ARCH=i686
SOURCE=http://10.0.2.2/bootstrap/bootstrap.tgz
INITSCR=http://10.0.2.2/bootstrap/inithead

## possible options
#SOURCE=cdrom
#INITSCR=server-scripts

#SOURCE=~tftp ....
#SOURCE=^http://server.somewhere.com/script-to-run
####################################
